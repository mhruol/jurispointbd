Our Services

JurisPoint provides comprehensive legal services to individuals, businesses, and corporate clients across Bangladesh. Our expertise spans corporate law, civil and criminal litigation, family law, banking, taxation, arbitration, and regulatory compliance.

  • Legal Drafting & Documentation: Drafting and reviewing contracts, agreements, pleadings, legal notices, policies, and transaction documents tailored to client objectives and regulatory requirements.
  • Filing & Court Representation: Filing cases, petitions, and applications, and representing clients before the Supreme Court of Bangladesh, subordinate courts, tribunals, and arbitration forums.
  • Advocacy & Litigation Support: Providing strategic advocacy and litigation support in civil, criminal, commercial, family, and administrative matters.
  • Legal Advisory & Consultancy: Delivering clear and practical legal advice on regulatory compliance, corporate governance, risk management, and domestic and cross-border transactions.
  • Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR): Advising and representing clients in arbitration, mediation, and negotiation to achieve efficient and confidential dispute resolution.
  • Regulatory & Compliance Guidance: Assisting businesses and individuals in complying with applicable local and international laws to mitigate legal, financial, and operational risks.
  • Due Diligence & Legal Audits: Conducting comprehensive legal due diligence and compliance audits of contracts, corporate structures, and transactions.
  • Immigration & Nationality Services: Advising individuals, foreign nationals, and corporate clients on visas, work permits, nationality matters, and immigration compliance in Bangladesh.
  • Family & Estate Advisory: Providing discreet and professional guidance on family law matters, divorce, custody, succession, inheritance, and estate planning.
  • Cross-Border & International Legal Support: Assisting multinational corporations, foreign investors, and international organizations with matters involving foreign jurisdictions, treaties, and international agreements.
  • Online Income Tax Return Filing: Assisting individuals and businesses with accurate and compliant online income tax return filing under Bangladeshi law.
